Elevator Theory-how it works

Elevator theory takes into account the production line or “lifecycle” of a cryptocurrency’s potential to succeed in the high growth, high volatility cryptocurrency universe

The theory is used to identify the creation of upcoming high quality cryptocurrencies with high utility and problem solving capability. On a practical level, elevator theory is the central pillar guiding the investment club to obtain access to the highest quality initial coin offerings at the earliest possible stage.
